About Me

I am a passionate Front-End Developer with a strong foundation in web application design and development. My journey began with a B.Tech in Computer Science from Bengal College of Engineering, Durgapur, where I honed my skills in C & C++.
Over the years, I have expanded my expertise to include a wide array of languages and technologies. I am proficient in JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, which I use to create stunning and responsive web interfaces. My proficiency in these languages has been further enhanced through my current role, where I have been actively involved in designing web applications since 2021.
In my pursuit of excellence, I have also delved into the realm of data structures in C++, which has not only deepened my understanding of programming concepts but has also equipped me with the skills to tackle complex problems efficiently.
My journey has led me to explore various frameworks and libraries, with ReactJS being my front-end framework of choice. My current endeavors also include mastering NodeJS, which complements my skills in ReactJS, enabling me to build full-stack applications seamlessly.
I am currently enhancing my skills through a Full Stack Web Development course at Coding Nexus, where I am refining my abilities to create robust and user-friendly web applications.
In addition to my technical skills, I am a diligent and enthusiastic team player, always eager to collaborate and contribute to innovative projects. My goal is to leverage my expertise and passion for web development to create engaging and dynamic digital experiences.
